This space is dedicated to feedback, ideas, and discussions about:
- The FLYEfit Mobile App
- API integrations
- Website functionality
- Kiosk and access terminal software
- In-gym digital systems

It is not for:
- Membership or account issues
- Billing questions
- Facility or equipment feedback
- Class timetables or gym operations

For those matters, please contact  
memberservice@flyefit.ie.

## How to Contribute

### 1. Use the Discussions Tab
All contributions happen through **Discussions**, not Issues or Pull Requests.

### 2. Choose the Right Category
Before posting, pick the most suitable category:
- App Feedback  
- API & Integrations  
- Website Feedback  
- Feature Ideas  
- Bug Reports (Digital Only)  
- General Tech Discussion  

### 3. Write Constructive and Clear Posts
Include:
- A clear title  
- A simple explanation of the feedback or issue  
- Steps to reproduce (if a bug)  
- Screenshots where useful  
- Platform and version (if applicable)

### 4. Avoid Confidential Information
Do not post:
- Account details  
- Personal data  
- Internal or sensitive operational information  

### 5. Respectful Participation
Remain constructive and on-topic. Non-digital topics may be removed to keep discussions focused.

## Moderation
Posts may be reorganised, recategorised, or removed to maintain clarity and scope.

Thank you for contributing to a better FLYEfit digital experience.
