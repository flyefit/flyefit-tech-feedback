# Code of Conduct

This repository is a public space for discussing improvements to FLYEfit digital products.  
To keep discussions productive and welcoming, the following rules apply.

## Expected Behaviour
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Stay on topic (digital/app/API/website/kiosk feedback only).
- Provide clear, actionable suggestions or reports when possible.
- Use professional and concise language.

## Unacceptable Behaviour
- Personal attacks or hostile language.
- Posting confidential or personal information.
- Posting content unrelated to digital products, including:
  - Membership issues
  - Account/billing problems
  - Gym equipment or facility feedback
  - Class timetables or operational matters
- Spamming or repeatedly posting the same request.
- Attempts to misuse the space as a customer support channel.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- Discussions
- Comments
- Shared content and links

## Moderation
Moderators may:
- Move or rename discussions
- Remove off-topic or inappropriate posts
- Issue warnings for repeated violations
- Restrict participation if necessary

## Member Service
For non-digital matters, contact  
memberservice@flyefit.ie.

Thank you for maintaining a positive and focused environment.
